Welcome

JIBAYATI Project is a digital legal platform specializing in providing tax and legal advice to individuals and institutions.

The platform aims to facilitate access to accurate legal information and tax services online in a modern and secure manner.

It enables users to submit their tax or legal inquiries and receive answers from certified specialists, in addition to providing online training services and electronic tracking of tax and administrative files.

The proposed solution involves digitizing tax and legal services to reduce physical travel and bureaucracy, and to improve the effectiveness of communication between citizens and legal or tax advisors..
